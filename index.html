Welcome to the FOSS Campus

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) empowers individuals and organizations by providing the freedom to use, modify, and distribute software. At our FOSS campus, we focus on fostering a collaborative environment where students and developers can learn, contribute, and grow by engaging with open-source projects and communities.

Code Café

Code Café is a weekly gathering where developers, enthusiasts, and learners exchange ideas, share their coding journeys, and showcase their achievements. It’s the perfect space to fuel your creativity and stay inspired.

Hack Hours

Hack Hours offers a dedicated time to collaborate with project maintainers and contributors. During these weekly virtual meet-ups, participants review ongoing projects, brainstorm ideas, and discuss technical challenges with guidance from experienced mentors.

Open Circles

Open Circles bring together people with shared interests in open-source technology. These informal learning groups dive into topics ranging from web development to cybersecurity, providing a collaborative environment to explore and grow.
